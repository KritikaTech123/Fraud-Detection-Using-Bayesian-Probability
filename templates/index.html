How the Method Works

The model starts with a prior fraud probability and updates it with evidence from selected anomalies. Each active anomaly contributes a likelihood ratio:

Posterior Odds = Prior Odds x Product of Likelihood Ratios

The posterior odds are converted to a final fraud probability and mapped to a risk level.

Risk Levels

  • Low Risk: score below 35%
  • Medium Risk: score from 35% to 69%
  • High Risk: score 70% and above

This is a lightweight demo, not a production fraud model.
